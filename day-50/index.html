Welcome to the FAQ section! Here, you'll find answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about my services, processes, and web development in general. If you have any other questions, feel free to reach out through the contact page.

I offer a wide range of web development services including custom web development, e-commerce solutions, CMS development, API integration, maintenance and support, and web development consultation. For more details, please visit the Services page.

The time it takes to build a website depends on the complexity and size of the project. A simple website can take a few weeks, while a more complex web application might take several months. During our initial consultation, I will provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

  • Frameworks/Libraries: React, Angular, Laravel
  • Tools/Technologies: Git, Docker, Webpack, MySQL

My process for starting a new project typically includes the following steps:

  1. Initial Consultation: We discuss your project requirements, goals, and budget.
  2. Proposal: I provide a detailed proposal outlining the project scope, timeline, and cost.
  3. Design Phase: We create wireframes and design mockups for your approval.
  4. Development Phase: I start building the website or application based on the approved designs.
  5. Testing: Thorough testing is conducted to ensure everything works perfectly.
  6. Launch: Once approved, the website is launched and made live.
  7. Maintenance: Ongoing support and maintenance are provided as needed.
